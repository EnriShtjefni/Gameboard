Instructions
  1. You start the game only when you roll a 6.
  2. If you roll a 6 you roll again up to three times
    and the number of tiles you are going to move adds up.
  3. If a player moves to another player's tile it
    sends that player back to the beginning
    of the board.
  4. There are interesting traps along the way.
  5. Have Fun!
